The Iced Tea

2. Two friends went to a resturant and ordered two iced teas, Lisa drank it quickly and Anna drank it slowly. 20 minutes later Anna died but Lisa survived. Both iced teas were poisoned but only Anna was killed. How?
ANSWER: The poison was in the ice, When Anna drank the iced tea the ice had already melted so the poison was already mixed inside while Lisa drank it before the poison melted.
